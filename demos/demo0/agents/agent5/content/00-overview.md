# Trianz — company overview

Trianz is a **Services as Software** company that accelerates enterprise business
transformations across **cloud, data, applications, and AI**. Founded in 2001 and
headquartered in Santa Clara, California, Trianz combines management consulting with
platform-powered delivery for Fortune 1000 enterprises and high-growth companies.

**Proof points:** 750+ clients globally · 3,000+ successful projects · 2,000+ employees
across four continents · 95%+ customer retention · partnerships with AWS (Premier Tier
Services Partner), Microsoft Azure, and Google Cloud.

**What makes Trianz different:** platform-driven transformation. Rather than purely
manual consulting, Trianz pairs expert services with its **Concierto** platform suite so
outcomes (migration, modernization, operations, FinOps, data intelligence) are faster,
repeatable, and measurable — "Services as Software."
