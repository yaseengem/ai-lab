# Trianz services and system-integration (SI) work

Trianz delivers across the full technology transformation lifecycle — from initial
migration and modernization through ongoing operations and optimization.

## Service lines

- **Cloud Migrations** — holistic application, infrastructure, and database migrations
  across AWS, Azure, and Google Cloud.
- **Application Modernization** — re-architecting legacy systems to cloud-native.
- **Cloud-Native Engineering** — designing, building, deploying, testing, and operating
  new platforms across the cloud software value chain.
- **Generative AI & Agentic Intelligence** — building GenAI and multi-agent solutions.
- **Data Insights & Agentic AI** — analytics, BI, and federated data intelligence.
- **Cloud Security Services** — assessment, tool deployment, 24/7 SOC operations, and
  multi-cloud security management with single-pane-of-glass visibility.
- **Managed Services** — 24/7 operations under SEI-CMM Level 5, ISO 27001, ITIL, and
  ISO 9001 quality standards.
- **Product Engineering** — full lifecycle digital product and application engineering.
- **ServiceNow Implementation** — 150+ service-management engagements, mostly Fortune 1000.

## System integration

Trianz's SI strength is breadth of partner integrations maintained within Concierto, so
clients get connected, proactive platforms rather than disconnected point tools.
